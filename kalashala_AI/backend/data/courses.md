# Weekend Basic to Advance Makeup Course

## Overview

The Weekend Basic to Advance Makeup Course is specially designed for students who prefer weekend learning. Classes are conducted only on Saturdays and Sundays, making it suitable for working professionals and students.

## Course Details

- **Duration:** 8 Weeks
- **Batch Type:** Weekend
- **Classes:** Saturday & Sunday
  
- **Batch Starts:** 18 July

## Makeup Topics Covered

Students will learn:

- Different Eye Makeup Looks
- Different Makeup Techniques
- Product Knowledge
- Makeup Tools Knowledge
- Dewy Makeup Base
- Matte Makeup Base
- Self Grooming
- Party Makeup Look
- Bridal Makeup Look

## Hair Styling Module

Students will also receive practical training in:

- 30 Hair Styles
- Curls
- Buns
- Braids
- Ponytail Hairstyles

## Saree Draping Module

Students will also learn:

- Saree Draping
- Box Folding

# Bridal Styling Master Class

## Overview

The Bridal Styling Master Class is a 30-day advanced training program designed for aspiring bridal stylists who want to specialize in professional bridal makeovers.

## Course Details

- **Duration:** 30 Days
- **Batch Starts:** 15 July 2026

## Topics Covered

Students will learn:

### Makeup

- 7 Professional Bridal Makeup Looks

### Hair Styling

- 30+ Different Hairstyles

### Saree Draping

- 10 Different Saree Draping & Pre-Pleating Styles

### Business Skills

- Digital Marketing Basics

## Course Benefits

Students receive:

- Certificate upon successful completion
- Branded products for practice sessions
- Lifetime free upgradation classes
- Free Makeup Kit worth ₹2,000 for the first 10 enrollments
